About Me

Hi I am Antony Manuel, a creative WEB / Graphic designer with 2 years of experience in this industry. I was born and brought up in the historic city of Kochi, Kerala (India). Well, I completed my degree in St. Albert’s College in 2011 and since then I’ve been busy with my hues and palette. I have gained design experience working in Alapatt Global. Later on I realized what was within, one thing lead to another and this turn of events. I should say this fortunate turn of events has turned me to a fully-fledged Creative designer without ever planning to become one. I love helping people build their ideas with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, Photoshop, Illustrator and Other web/graphic designing tools..!. I expertise in Creative design, Branding, HTML & CSS, and Responsive Web design. As a designer, I oversee the process from start to finish, from concept and developing to the final product and consistently motivating myself to get the best.
